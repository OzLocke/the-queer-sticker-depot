The Queer Sticker Depot

Here you will find a collection of sticker designs that you are free* to use to spread support and awareness for queer folks around the world.

Click an image to go to the folder for that image on git. From there you can click on the link to the file type you want** and then click download to the right just above the image.

You may also submit your own designs for consideration via the "Submit designs" button bellow

* See Terms
** Most sticker printers will appreciate you providing them with the PDF
